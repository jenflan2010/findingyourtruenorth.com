About the Platform

A wider map for a complex problem

Most approaches to mental health address only part of the picture. Finding Your True North brings together what the evidence across multiple fields is telling us: that anxiety and depression have causes that are biological, relational, metabolic, and deeply human all at once.

This platform was built by a clinician with over twenty years of experience, grounded in the research, and shaped by the consistent truth that the most powerful moment in any healing journey is the one where a person finally stops blaming themselves.

01

The Nervous System

Trauma is not primarily a memory problem. It is a body problem. Understanding how the nervous system holds experience and how it can be helped to release it is the foundation of everything this platform offers.

02

Metabolic Psychiatry

The brain is a physical organ with physical requirements. The emerging science of metabolic psychiatry is showing us that what we eat directly shapes how we feel, in ways the mental health field is only beginning to fully appreciate.

03

Lifestyle as Medicine

Sleep, movement, sunlight, and connection are not peripheral self-care habits. They are the biological floor of mental health, and the evidence for their impact is stronger than most people realise.

04

Meaning and Coherence

The ache for purpose, connection, and a felt sense of belonging in your own life is not neurotic restlessness. It is a signal from a system that knows what it is built for. This platform takes that signal seriously.
